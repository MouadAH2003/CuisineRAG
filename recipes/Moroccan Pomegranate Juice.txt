Moroccan Pomegranate Juice Recipe

Adjust to taste of the pomegranate juice to your liking and according to the variety you have in hand. You may need to add more or less sugar. The recipe below is just a guideline.

Prep Time10 minutes minsChill Time1 hour hrTotal Time1 hour hr 10 minutes mins

Course: DrinksCuisine: Moroccan
 Yield: 2 servings
Calories: 213kcal
Author: Nada Kiffa | Taste of Maroc
Ingredients1 lb pomegranate seeds1 tbsp orange blossom water, or to taste1 tbsp sugar or honey, or to taste1 cup water or orange juice (optional)1 tbsp lemon juice optional; use if the fruit is very sweet)US Customary - Metric
InstructionsIn a food processor or a juicer, blend all the ingredients for a few seconds. Strain the juice and discard the seeds.Chill the juice then shake before serving.
Notes
The pomegranate juice without orange keeps for up to 4 days in the fridge. The version with orange juice will keep for up to 2 days.
